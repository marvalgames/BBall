SCHEDULE SCREEN

View the days scheduled games

Click sim to have the computer instantly play the scheduled game

Click coach to coach the scheduled game yourself

Click coach to view the box score for any games that have that already taken place

Sim Day – All games will be automatically simulated by the computer until all games scheduled for that day are completed.

Sim Week – All games will be automatically simulated by the computer until all the games scheduled over the next week are completed.

Example: You click Simulate Week after the second game on February 3rd. Simulation will then stop immediately after the last game on February 10th (7 days later)

Simulate Month – All games will be automatically simulated by the computer until all the games scheduled over the next month are completed.

Example: You click Simulate Month after the second game on February 3rd. Simulation will then stop immediately after the last game on March 3rd (One calendar month later)

Sim until All Star – All games will be automatically simulated by the computer until the all star game is scheduled

Sim Regular Season - All games will be automatically simulated by the computer until the end of the season when the Start Playoffs Button will be enabled

You will be prompted to stop simming if a team is set to coach the games from the Franchise Settings
