ALLSTAR WEEKEND

The all star weekend takes place at a random date sometime after the halfway point of the season

Note: It may even take place the same day as a day with games scheduled, if the league schedule does not include at least two off days after the halfway point

The All Star Weekend button from the schedule screen will be enabled when it is scheduled

The game selects the participants for the dunk contest based on ratings and participants for the three point contest based on stats and ratings

To change participants for the allstar game highlight a player from the allstar list, select the player you wish to include the game and click the Change button

After completion of the contests click exit to go to return to the schedule screen.

You can then coach or sim the rookie game and the allstar game
