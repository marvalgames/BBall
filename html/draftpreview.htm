Rookie Draft

Use this command allows a rookie draft of non-professional players.

If used during the season you’ll see a list of the rookies that will be available for drafting when the season is over

The draft can begin after using the End Season command.

Selecting a different team name will allow you to see how that teams lead scout views the rookies in the draft.

Each teams scout will rate players differently based on several ratings:

Scoring

Shooting

Getting to the FT line

Passing/Ball Handling

Rebounding

Defense

Talent Projection

Skill Projection

Intangible Projection

Click the Column headers to sort the players by category. Clicking on players will sort them based on the scouts overall evaluation of the player.

Teams that are play controlled will draft for themselves, however you can change that by selecting a team and clicking on simulate.

Computer controlled teams General Managers(GM) use the scouting report and their own evaluation to determine a choice. The GM need rating is used to determine if a player is selected based more on talent or a more on teams position need.

Select a team to view that teams draft board (rankings). With scouts on, each team will rank the players differently.

The draft board order is determined by the teams GM

Click the Draft Lottery to set the draft order. The computer will determine the top three picks using the pro basketball style lottery system and the remaining teams that failed to make the playoffs will pick in the order of worst to best record. The worst the team was during the season the higher the likely hood of receiving an early pick. Only teams that failed to make the playoffs are part of the lottery.

The draft will proceed automatically until the end of the draft or until a player controlled teams turn.

Click the Draft Button to begin the draft. The draft will proceed automatically until the end of the draft or until a play controlled teams turn.

Click the Trade Button to trade during the draft (Available after the lottery)

Click the Next Selection to resume the draft one pick at a time

Click the Speed Draft  to complete the draft, drafting will still stop if a player controlled team's choice is up next

To draft a player, simply click Draft Selected Player and the currently highlighted player will be drafted.

View the Rookie report of the highlighted player as reported by the scout by double clicking. The GM will use this report to help determine the draft board.

After the completion of a draft, the Save Draft Button will be available. You can click it to end the draft.

Click Cancel to exit the draft at anytime.

The rookies that are on the draft board will be the same ones as those that will appear when selecting The Rookie Draft Command again.

When the draft has been saved the players will not be placed on any rosters until first using the Training Camp command to set the rosters.
