STAFF MEMBER PROFILES

GENERAL

WIN(W) – Career wins as a staff member

LOSS (L) – Career losses

PCT – Career winning percentage

PLAYOFFS (PO) – Career playoff appearances

RINGS (RI) – Career championships

LOYALTY – Loyalty to current team affects risk of resigning

EVALUATION (SCOUTING)

TALENT (TA) – Projection of a players eventual talent level (talented players tend to become effective scorers and shooters)

SKILL (SR) – Projection of a players eventual overall skill (skilled players tend to develop more of an all around game)

INTANGIBLES (IN) – Projection of a players eventual work ethic (players with high intangibles tend to improve more dramatically and will improve consistently)

SCORING (SC) – Ability to evaluate a players ability to create shots and draw fouls

SHOOTING (SH) – Ability to evaluate a players ability to make shots and freethrows

REBOUNDING (RE) – Ability to evaluate a players offensive and defensive rebounding

BALL HANDLING (BH) – Ability to evaluate a players passing and turnovers

DEFENSE (DE) – Ability to evaluate a players shot blocking and steals

DRAFT BY NEED (GM ONLY) – Tendency to draft by position needs versus overall talent

Coaches use their evaluation to decide a roster in training camp

GM’s use their evaluation to decide on trades

Scouts evaluations are used primarily in the rookie draft. The GM will then use the evaluation to choose the player

PLAYER DEVELOPMENT (COACHES ONLY)

TALENT – Coaches ability to maximize a players talent

SKILL – Coaches ability to improve a players overall skill level

INTANGIBLES – Coaches ability to motivate a player

SCORING – Coaches ability to improve a players scoring ability

SHOOTING – Coaches ability to improve a players overall shooting

REBOUNDING – Coaches ability to develop a players rebounding

BALL HANDLING – Coaches ability to improve a players passing and minimize

turnovers

DEFENSE – Coaches ability to improve a players defense

CONDITIONING (CO) – Affects player fatigue during games

PATIENCE – Used in training camp. Patient coaches tend to keep young undeveloped players who show talent

GAME COACHING (COACHES ONLY)

All ratings improve effectiveness and chances of the proper play being executed when calling plays

The higher rated coaches will have players who execute the called play more often

OFFENSE

OUTSIDE (O) – Improved outside shooting

DRIVING (D) – Improved driving and penetration

POST (P) – Improved inside/post scoring

TRANSITION (T) – Improved fast break chances

DEFENSE

OUTSIDE (OD) – Decreased opponent outside shooting

DRIVING (DD) – Decreased opponent driving/penetration

POST (PD) – Decreased opponent post/inside scoring

TRANSITION (TD) – Decreased opponent fast break chances
