Statistics

Use this command to display the computer generated stats, actual stats and ratings for all players or teams

Select Players, Team Offense or Team Defense

Simulated – stats generated in the current season

Playoffs – stats generated during the current post season

Ratings – Ratings of players (Available only when Players View is selected)

Actual – Actual season stats of players (not active in career play mode – available in players only)

Contracts - age, height, weight, year by year contract info

 

All stats and ratings are sortable by clicking the appropriate column

Select High to Low or Low to High

You can also select to view by positions

Double Click for a Player Report Player Report for a detailed look at the currently highlighted player.

The number under injury is the amount of games the player will remain out because of injury.

Special abbreviations under ratings

OO – Usually excellent jump shooters. These players fight through screens, move without the ball and in some cases their height allows them to shoot over people

DO – Ability to both dribble and drive for scoring tries. Point guards usually are the best at this.

PO – Measures the inside game of a player. Usually higher for power forwards and centers.

TO – Measures the ability of a player to run the floor and finish on the first break.

OD – Fight screens, prevent shooters by denying them the ball and have the size to distract shots.

DD – Stops drives by not getting beat off the dribble.

PD – Ability to deny the ball down low and to stop the other players inside game.

TD – Measures the ability of a player to get back on defense and to either stop or slow down the fast break.

Ratings

Use this  view the ratings for all players in the current league.

In career play the ratings will be adjusted according to the scout of the team roster displayed.

Select the team to view the ratings for all players on that team.

Ratings are determined by taking the league best and giving that player the highest rating.

All other players are rated proportionately to the league best. The rating is adjusted for minutes played. These ratings can also be viewed from the Player Report

If you have elected to use your scouts using the Settings Command these ratings will vary based on their evaluation of a player.
