Career Play Projections:

These projections effect the development curve of players.

The ratings are randomly created and are reported differently based on the scout.

The players tend to improve until they reach their random prime year (usually 26-28)

and steadily decline afterwards.

Talent - stronger effect on scoring categories

Skill - stronger effect on passing and rebounding

Intangibles - players work ethic and desire effecting individual and team performance. These players usually reach their prime later than most players.
