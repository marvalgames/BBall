Scheduler Command (Tools menu)

Use this command to create or edit a complete schedule for the current league.

The Reset Button starts the season over. All player stats and game results will be lost but the schedule will remain intact.

In order to make changes to the schedule the current schedule must not have started league play. If the season is underway you must use the Reset Button to start the season over.

 

RESET BUTTON – The reset button can only be clicked when you want to erase all the game results of the current schedule, thereby starting the current league schedule over again.

The schedule cannot be edited unless the league currently being played is not underway and no game results have been posted. In order to edit the schedule you must first click the reset button. If a league game is currently being played it will revert to an exhibition game.

CREATING A SCHEDULE – To create a schedule, first select the day and month that you would like to play a particular game. From the teams list, highlight the teams that you would like playing on that particular day and month. Next decide which team you would like to play at home and as the visitor. Once you have decided click the appropriate button.

After completing this procedure you should see the two teams scheduled to play each other listed in the appropriate home and visitor boxes. Repeat the same process for every game to be played that day.

REMOVE FROM SCHEDULE – To remove a game or a team from a schedule, simply highlight the team or teams that you wish to delete. Then click the Remove Button. To exchange the position of any two teams in the schedule, just click on the two teams that you want to swap places.

AUTO SCHEDULE - This button is activated before the start of any season. Select this to create your own custom schedule for your league with the Auto Scheduler
