Exhibition Game Team Select

Used to select match ups for exhibition games involving the current league.

Highlight the Visitor and Home teams.

Computer

Checked - Computer gives coaching control to the computer coach

Unchecked - it's up to the user to call the shots.

Swap Home and Visitor

Checked -The home team is now the visitor and vice versa.

Unchecked - Normal

This is useful when you want to play a series of games featuring the same teams but be able to switch whose home.

Home Court Advantage

Checked - The home team has an advantage over the visitor.

Unchecked - The teams play on a neutral floor with no edge to either side.
