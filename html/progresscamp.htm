Training Camp Command

Use this command to determine your roster before season play.

This command is only available after a Rookie Draft has occurred.

To release a player from a player controlled team, select a player and click the Release Player Button.

View a Player Report by double clicking the selected player.

Click on the Column Headers to sort the players on a particular team by categories.

Note: The ratings are based on the COACHES ability to evaluate a player.

If any players have retired they will be displayed under the Set Computer Teams Button.

On rare occasions a player will retire prematurely because of personal circumstances.

Some players will no longer be available because their poor play did not warrant an invite to training camp.

Click the Set Computer Teams Button after releasing any unwanted players.

The computer coaches based on their evaluation of a player will set the rosters for simulate controlled teams.

The computer also uses a project rating to decide to keep a player based on potential and age or to go with an established player regardless of age.

Immediately after this the Hall of Fame Selections Button will be enabled. Click this button to determine if any players will be entering the Hall of Fame.

Now you can click the End Training Camp Button to save the rosters to be used for the next season.

Retirements are random and fairly rare. Better players tend to retire rather than play too long. Occasionally a player will unexpectedly retire before their prime

Use the Cancel Training Camp Button if you don’t like what just happened or who retired. (Cheat)

Use the Progress Button to see how the player rating have changed compared to last season
