STAFF HIRING

Use this command to view the profiles of a team’s staff members (Coach, Scout, and Assistant General Manager)

This command also allows for the hiring and firing of staff members immediately after choosing End Season

Select Scout, Coach or Assistant GM

When selecting Coach, you can select the in-game ratings as well

For a complete profile including explanations of abbreviations, select a member and double click

Only after ending a season  will the View Firings Button becomes active

Select this button to view resignations, firings, and retirements for the just completed season.

To sort categories, click on a column label.

Staff members can only be fired if a team is play controlled. However a play controlled teams members can still retire or resign.

To replace a member, select the member and click the Offer Job Button. The team must be play controlled and only 1 offer to a Scout, Coach, or GM. Offers are only available after selecting view hirings. All play controlled teams can make offers.

After making offers, if any, select the View Hirings Button to view the current round of hirings.

Staff members will resign if they are unhappy with the current status of their team. The better members are more likely to resign in hopes of landing a better job. The better a teams roster and record, the less likely of a resignation. Scouts, Coaches, and GM’s weight different categories when resigning or accepting a job offer. Firings are less likely with patient owners.

If the Continue Hirings Button is active, you can continue making offers and repeat the process. Continue until all teams have made their hirings.

The End Hiring Period Button will then be enabled. Click to save the changes.

The staff members will all age and their skills will change over the years. Keep an eye out because different members improve at different rates.

Click cancel to not save the changes.
