Receive Trade Proposals

Trade proposals occasionally occur during the course of league play.

Teams that have been selected to receive trade offers from the Settings Button will be

given the option to select or decline a proposed trade before some regular season league games.

Click Accept to say yes to the trade.

Click Decline to say no to the trade.

 

 Trading Block

Use this command to start a short trading period between teams in the league.

Click Get Offers to get offers for the selected team

Click Add Player to Block to let other teams no that this player is available. The offers will include players on the block.

Players on the block may have their happiness and loyalty affected

Check Computer Team Trading allowed will allow trades to take place among the other teams as well. Useful to simulate the occasional trading frenzies that occasionally occur before the draft or the trade deadline
