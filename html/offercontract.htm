NEGOTIATE CONTRACTS

Contract Extensions

Contract extensions are available from the team page only

Extensions are available to players in the last year of a contract and during the season only

Extensions are a maximum of 5 additional years.

The exceptions are not available for use in extensions

The offered salaries must fit into the offseason cap amounts

If a player receives a poor offer they will let it be known

 If a player receives too many poor offers the player will no longer wish to negotiate

 

Offseason Offers

Offer contracts for up to six years

The whole numbers entered are translated to dollar figures after an approved offer is submitted

Number are divided by 100 to set an amount in millions

Example:

966 is divided by 100

Result is 9.66 or

9.66 million

OFFER SETTINGS

Normal Negotiation

Allows editing of any year amounts

Maximum Salary

Will fill in the yearly offers with the highest amount allowed for each year.

The maximum amount is based on Salary Cap Rules and the owner designated cap if Finances is enabled

Minimum Salary

Will fill in the yearly offers with the lowest amount allowed for just one year.

The minimum amount is based on Salary Cap Rules

Minimum Exception

Designate a one year 1.10 million contract

One allowed per free agency period

Clicking Normal Negotiation and then submitting an offer does not qualify as an exception and is the same as a normal offer

Mid Level Exception

Designate a one year 2.25 million contract

One allowed per free agency period

Clicking Normal Negotiation and then submitting an offer does not qualify as an exception and is the same as a normal offer

TOP THREE

The players top three choices to play for are listed in order are determined by the factors described below

CONTRACT FACTORS

loyalty - Players loyalty to the team last played for

winner - Importance of team success in the just completed season

play time - Importance of getting quality playing time

security - Importance of a long term deal versus a short deal that will allow the player to test the market more often.

coach - Importance of the quality of the coach and the personality of the coach

tradition - Importance of the success of the franchise throughout its history. Also factors in the current value of the team when Finances are on
