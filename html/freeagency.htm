Free Agency Period

Use this command to view player free agent status & requests. During the off-season this command is used to sign free agents during the free agency period.

Free Agency Salary Rules:

Every team has a soft cap. The cap salaries of all players can total no more than that amount in any season. Soft Caps allow teams to go over the cap limit by using salary cap exceptions.

Note: adjust the soft cap from Franchise Settings

Salary Cap Exceptions:

  1. First round draft pick rookies are automatically signed to contracts defined by the rookie salary scale with the 1st overall pick in round 1 having the largest salary. The scale changes depending upon the number of teams in the league. Second round picks receive 2 year contracts
  2. Each year a team can sign a player to a minimum exception. The minimum exception is a 1 year contract at 1.1 million.
  3. Each year a team can sign a player to a midlevel exception. This midlevel exception is a 1 year contract at 4.5 million.
  4. Teams can sign players to league minimum contracts. The minimum is determined by a players years of service. Players with 10 or more years of service can sign for 1 million dollars.
  5. Teams can use the Bird Rule Exception on Players with Bird Rights. This rule allows teams to go over the cap to sign the free agents on their own teams. Players eligible for Bird Rights have not left one team to sign with another team during the last 3 years.

Other Rules:

  1. Contracts can not exceed 6 years

  2. Raises can not exceed 10% for players without Bird rights

  3. Raises can not exceed 15% for players with Bird rights

  4. Contracts must not exceed the maximum salary in the first year of a contract. The maximum is determined by years of service. Players with 10 or more years of service can receive a 14 million maximum.

Owner Salary Cap – The salaries of all players on a team cannot total more than the owner salary cap in any season. The owner’s cap is determined by both a team’s current value and the owners spending rating (hidden). The cap changes each year based on the success of the team and profit of the team. It is possible for the owners cap to be less that soft cap amount. You can exceed the owner cap by offering minimum contracts only. To disable the owner cap restrictions turn off finances from the Franchise Settings Button

Explanation of Free Agency Period – Free agency period occurs between the Rookie Draft and Training Camp. You can view this screen any other time but you will not be able to offer contracts.

Each stage allows teams to offer players a contract as long as the total of the offered salaries adhere to the cap rules. Example:  You are 20 million under the cap and then offer 2 players, 10 million dollars a season, bringing you to the cap amount. You can no longer offer contracts to any more players unless the rules allow you to go over the cap. In this case you may have players with bird rights and then you could offer those players money and exceed the cap, also if you have an exception available you can still offer one player the exception amount (not more than one because both players can not sign). You can always offer players the minimum amounts. Also the number of offers is limited to the amount of roster slots available, offers exceeding this amount will be ignored when the requests are processed.

 Highlight the player that you wish to offer a contract to, then highlight the team that is negotiating. The team must be set to player. Then select the Negotiate Contract Button to arrange your offer.

By clicking Ok from the Negotiate Screen you will submit an offer that will be listed in the offers box. To override the offer, simply select a new player and repeat the above listed process.

Signings will be listed in the Signings Box.

Subsequent rounds follow the same procedure except the Begin Free Agency Period Button is now the Continue Free Agency Period Button.

The negotiate contract button will be disabled for computer teams and for currently signed players.

Complete Free Agency Period button – Click when player controlled teams are finished with negotiations. Computer teams will continue to bid on players as necessary

The Complete Free Agency Period button will also be enabled when all computer controlled teams have filled the rosters to their satisfaction. Click to complete the free agency period.

Explanation of List Box Controls

POS – Players Primary Position

YRS PRO – Players years of service (used to determine minimum and maximum salaries)

BIRD – Years player has not moved from one team to another-via free agency. (Minimum of 3 years is necessary for Bird Rights).

CONTRACT YRS – Total years of current contract

YRS LEFT – Years remaining on contract.

Years Left is determined by years left on contract during the current season. This means players with 1 year left are considered free agents during the signing period.

AMOUNT – Total Salary remaining on contract

YRS SOUGHT – Estimate of contract years a player seeks.

Amount – Estimate of salary a player seeks

YRS AGREED – Years of contract for a player signed during the Free Agency Period

AMOUNT – Total salary of a player signed during the Free Agency Period

STATUS – Signed: Player under contract

Team Name: Player who just signed

Unsigned: Player remains a Free Agent

  1. All players contracts are guaranteed. If a player is released while under contract, the contract continues to count towards the salary cap of the team until either (a) The end of the contract or (b) The player is signed by another team.

FROM – If player has left via free agency the team that the player left is shown here

VIEW OPTIONS

All Players – List players under contract for upcoming season

Free Agents – List players who are free agents prior to the free agency period

Signings – List players that were signed during free agency (Rookies are automatically signed)
