Settings Button

Use this command to select special options for the current league.

Select the team and then set the following options:

INDIVIDUAL TEAM OPTIONS

note: these options have no effect in exhibition games

Save Play by Play

checked - Play by Play is saved immediately after the selected team league game.

You can then review the play by play by using the

View Saved Play by Play menu command

Due to the size of each play by play log in the file that is kept (leaguename.pbp) We suggest using this sparingly. If you are controlling one team and are saving the teams play by play for a season, the file size will be around 8mb for a 82 game season.

Save Box Scores

checked - Game Stats are saved immediately after the selected team league game.

You can then review the game stats by using the

Tools Saved Box Scores

Receive Trade Offers

checked - Selected team will receive trade offers from all non checked teams during the season. Player Trading must be set to on.

Note: If you select this for all the teams in the league no team will receive offers because no teams will be left to propose trades.

This option allows you basically to just be the general manager as you can select to simulate your teams games and wait for any offers.

Player Coaches All Games

checked – Player will coach all the games for selected team. Simulating of games will always stop before any games involving the selected team

Player Controls Off Court Decisions

checked – Player will control all trades, cuts, signings, finance etc. for selected team. When selecting this team in all game screens the control for the team will be set to player. Simulating of games will not stop automatically

Display Box Score After Each Game

checked – After completion of any game played by the selected team. The Box-Score of that game will automatically be displayed on the screen.

SCOUTING OPTIONS

Use scout of currently highlighted team for all player scouting reports

checked - All Player Reports will use the selected scout to evaluate the player..

Recommended when controlling one team. Allows the player to view the scouting reports for all teams using the player controlled teams scout.

Do not use scout ratings for teams

checked - The actual ratings will be used to evaluate players. Coaches, scouts and GMs will all perform at the same level.

unchecked - The evaluation and development and coaching of players will be based on a teams staff. (If you choose to use a single scout (above) the same scout will be used for all teams. Each team will still use their own coach and GM.)

GAME RULES

3 pointers off

checked - no 3 point shot line

unchecked - 3 point line

3 ft on 3 pt off

checked - 2 free throws on fouled 3 point attempts

unchecked - 3 free throws on fouled 3 point attempts

SCORING

use the slider to either lower or raise the scoring in the current league.

This is accomplished by increasing or decreasing the average possesions in a game per team.

Very Low will cause about a 15-20% decrease in scoring.

Very High will increase scoring about 15-20%.

GENERAL OPTIONS

Computer Trading

checked - Computer teams will trade with each other.

unchecked - Recommended when replaying seasons of the past

Salaries Matching in Trades

Checked - Trades made by the player or the computer will follow salary cap rules as follows

  1. One or both teams over the cap: Total Salaries of players involved in trades must be within 15% of each other plus .1 million

Example:

Player A on New Jersey has a salary of 10 million

Player B on Chicago has a salary of 12 million

Player B has a salary 20% greater than player A so the trade is not possible

  1. Trades are allowed regardless of salary if both teams are under the cap as the result of the trade

Computer teams will also weigh salary when making trades and signing or cutting players.

Teams with a higher value will factor money less in its decision making.

Unchecked – Trades need not follow the salary rules

Free Agency

checked - Free Agency screen enabled and free agent signing period completion required after each season

unchecked – Free Agency not enabled. Play without free agency

Finances

checked – Budget Sheet and Proposal screen enabled and price setting and proposal period completion required after each season

unchecked – Budget Sheet and Proposal screen not enabled. Play without finances. The amount of money to be used in Free Agency will only be limited by the Salary Cap rules and not by the owner of the team.

Injury Notify For Player Controlled Teams

checked - Game simulation will stop and notify of injuries of games greater than the selected number for all teams that have the off court decision control set to player..

Allow Assistant GM to Fill Rosters of Player Controlled Teams

checked – when accepting trade offers the assistant GM will sign players it feels the team needs to complete a roster before continuing.

Unchecked – the player has to fill roster slots that may have opened up as a result of trades

Click OK to save the changes.

Click Cancel to exit without saving.
