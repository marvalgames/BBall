Used to called timeouts during game play.

Seven timeouts are allowed for each team, plus one 20 second timeout per half per team.

Full timeouts can be used to give players a rest and to set up an offensive play.

Plays are more likely to succeed after a timeout (less so after a 20)

The team on offense can call a timeout at any time. Otherwise their must be a stoppage in play.

The unavailable team or teams will be grayed out.

Just mark the 20 second check box to call a 20 second TO when clicking the appropriate team button.

Regardless of the number of timeouts called a team can have no more than four full timeouts at the beginning of the fourth period.

Overtime periods allow three timeouts per session.
