Defensive matchups

Used to set defensive matchups during player games.

To set the matchups click the team you are controlling and you will see the current matchups.

To change the defender just highlight the offensive player in the player box then double click the player in the defender box that you want guarding the selected offensive player.

One can choose to defend a player in several ways.

Outside(O) is selected when you want a defender guarding a player close to take away outside shots.

Drive(D) gives a player space but can take away penetration.

Post(P) allows a player to play strong and try to deny the player a post up inside shot.

Double team is used to control an offensive player by always having someone help out on the play to shut down the player.

To select a defensive style just click on the scroller of the small box next to the player that you want defended that way and it will show your options.
