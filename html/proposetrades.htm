Trade Players

Used to trade players, sign unprotected players and release players to free agency.

This command is unavailable while a game is in progress. (Click Play - Exit Game)

To initiate a trade, highlight and double click on the players that you want to involve in a trade. Their names will appear in the smaller boxes below. Make sure that at least one of the teams included is in player controlled mode.

By default, the teams you selected as player-controlled, using the Play Options Command are considered player controlled when trading. The control can be changed allowing you to make trades for any team(Cheat)

Trades involving two play-controlled teams are automatically accepted.

Trades with just one player controlled team, must first be accepted by the computer’s general manager.

If you have elected to use your scouts using the Settings Command the

computer controlled teams will evaluate a player based on the scout evaluation and the GM evaluation.

The edit boxes on the bottom of the screen display the current payroll of each team and the total salaries of the players involved in the proposed trade

If factor salaries in trades in enabled the total salaries involved in trade must be within 15% of each other plus .1 million or both teams must be under the salary cap for a trade to be allowed

Free agents can be signed by highlighting the player that you wish to acquire from the free agent list and by then clicking the SIGN button. You can release players by first highlighting the player’s name in the list that appears above the release button. Then click on the RELEASE button.

Click the Recommend Button to find players to go after in a trade

When you have completed all your trades and transactions simply click OK to accept the trades.

Click CANCEL to exit without any changes.
